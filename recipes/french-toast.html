French Toast Recipe

Description

It's easier than you think to make restaurant-quality French toast in the comfort of your own kitchen â€“ you just need a skillet, a few staple ingredients, and a good recipe. That's where we come in!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
